Samsung Galaxy S24

The Samsung Galaxy S24 is a cutting-edge smartphone that combines sleek design with powerful features. With its 6.4 inches Super AMOLED display, octa-core Exynos XYZ processor, and stunning camera setup, it delivers an exceptional mobile experience for users who demand performance and style.

Specifications

  1. Display: 172.5mm (6.8" full rectangle) / 172.2mm (6.8" rounded corners)
  2. Processor: Deca-Core, Exynos 2400
  3. RAM: 8GB
  4. Storage: 512GB
  5. Camera: 48MP + 12MP + 5MP Triple Rear, 32MP Front
  6. Battery: 4000mAh
  7. OS: Android
  8. SIM Type: Dual-SIM
